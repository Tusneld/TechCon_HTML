History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2018 as a small meetup for local developers interested in emerging technologies. Over the years, it has grown into a major international conference, hosting thousands of attendees and hundreds of speakers from every corner of the globe. Our initial focus on open-source software has expanded to include cutting-edge topics like AI ethics and sustainable computing practices.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation, connect brilliant minds, and drive positive change in the technology sector. We aim to create an inclusive platform where diverse perspectives are celebrated, and future technologies are responsibly explored. Our core principles revolve around knowledge sharing, ethical development, and community building.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Anya Sharma

Dr. Sharma is a leading expert in explainable AI (XAI). Her talks at TechCon 2022 focused on making complex AI models more transparent and trustworthy, profoundly influencing industry standards for AI auditing.

Mr. Ben Carter

Mr. Carter, co-founder of the Nebula Cloud Platform, delivered the keynote in 2021 on the power of community-driven development and the future of serverless architecture.
